Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

Simple Promise: We don't collect, store, sell, or share any of your personal data. Period.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • We don't share your information with anyone
  • We don't use cookies or analytics

What Data Is Stored

The only data stored is your high score, which is saved locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your computer.

Permissions

This extension requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your high score locally
  • Tabs: To open links when using redirect mode

Third-Party Services

This extension may connect to Firebase for game configuration updates. No personal data is sent to Firebase.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
